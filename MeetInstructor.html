Meet Your Instructor

With over five years of dedicated yoga practice, I bring a balanced, compassionate, and grounded approach to teaching. My classes emphasize mindful movement, breath awareness, and alignment to support both physical strength and inner calm. Drawing from various styles including Hatha, Vinyasa, and restorative yoga, I aim to create an inclusive and supportive space for students of all levels. My goal is to help others reconnect with their bodies, reduce stress, and cultivate a deeper sense of well-being on and off the mat.
