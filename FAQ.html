Frequently Asked Questions

Can beginners practice yoga?

Yes, our classes are beginner friendly. We welcome yogis of all levels.

Do I need to be flexible to practice yoga?

No, you do not need to be flexible to practice yoga. You can actually build your flexibility by practicing consistently.

When will I see the results?

Results are different for everyone, depending on your personal goals you would like to achieve the timing will vary. Of course the more you practice, the more benefits you will experience. One day a week is a great place to start when building a habit of consistency.

Is yoga just stretching or what?

While stretching is part of it, yoga is a mindful practice that combines balance, strength and patience to achieve physical results.

What should I wear to yoga?

You should wear something comfortable that is also dynamic for movement.

What are the different yoga classes provided?

We offer Hatha yoga, Vinyasa yoga, and Restorative yoga. You can find out more about types of yoga practices here.
